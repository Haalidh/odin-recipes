WATTALAPAM

Home

Description

This is one of srilaka's most prominent sweet item.

"If you had a spoon,you want stop until you finish it."

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (500g) grated kithul jaggery or dark brown molasses sugar
  • 2 cups (500ml) thick, full-fat coconut milk
  • 10 large eggs
  • ¼ cup water
  • ½ tsp lime juice (optional, prevents hardening)
  • 8 cardamom pods, lightly bruised, or ¼ tsp cardamom powder
  • ½ tsp grated nutmeg
  • Pinch of salt
  • 50g roasted cashews, for garnish

Steps

  1. Melt the jaggery
  2. Add spices and cool
  3. Prepare the egg mixture
  4. Combine the mixtures
  5. Strain the custard
  6. Steam the watalappam
  7. Cool and chill
  8. Serve and garnish
